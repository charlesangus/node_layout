     a side subtree root extends LEFT of its spine node when the side root is wider
     than the spine node). Advance formula uses both effective_widths[i+1] (next
     node's rightward extent) AND left_extents[i] (current node's leftward extent)
     to ensure a full step_x gap between adjacent side subtree bounding boxes.
     centering_offset clipping bug (max(0,...)) also fixed.
  2. Leftmost Dot placement: Dot is now placed at spine Y (vertically centered on
     the leftmost spine node), X-centered under the upstream subtree root. The
     upstream subtree is placed ABOVE the Dot (not to the left at spine Y as before).
     Wire now routes: upstream root → vertical drop → Dot at spine Y → horizontal
     run → leftmost spine node.
  3. Place Only translates entire subtrees: both side inputs (slots 1+) AND the
     leftmost input[0] subtree are now translated as rigid units (all nodes in
     collect_subtree_nodes moved by same delta), not just the subtree root.
